Certified

This product is certified by our supplier, Stanley/Stella as GOTS, Oeko-Tex Standard 100, Fair Wear and Vegan Approved. Since we are not a certified company yet, unfortunately we can’t market these products as certified ourselves. We pay great attention to the materials we choose, to make sure they are healthy for you and the Planet, even though we can’t gain any competitive advantage through certificates. We just find it the right thing to do and we want to be as transparent as we possibly can.

Recycled

This product contains recycled fibers, which supports the circular economy model, where all raw materials are kept in use as long as possible. Recycling means conserving natural resources and reducing waste, while preventing it from the landfills and incinerators.

Sustainable

This product is produced in a sustainable way, which means that it is created in a way that can be sustained for this and future generations, while protecting both Planet and People producing it. It is produced by a Fair Wear Foundation Leader Stanley/Stella which, by using organic cotton and recycled polyester, helps sustain natural resources and improve the livelihood of people involved in its production.

End of Lifecycle - recyclable
